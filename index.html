How to Get a Home Loan with Low Interest

Overview: Getting a home loan with low interest in 2025 requires good credit, smart comparisons, and the right negotiation strategy with banks or NBFCs.

‚úÖ Tips to Get the Lowest Home Loan Interest Rate


  • Maintain a High Credit Score: Aim for a CIBIL score above 750. Higher scores get lower interest rates.
  • Compare Multiple Lenders: Use platforms like Paisabazaar, BankBazaar, or directly visit bank websites to check current offers.
  • Go for a Shorter Tenure: Shorter loan tenures often have lower interest rates and save you money in the long run.
  • Choose a Floating Rate Loan: In a declining interest rate market, floating rates can be more affordable than fixed rates.
  • Apply with a Co-Applicant: Adding a co-applicant with stable income and good credit improves your eligibility and lowers the rate.
